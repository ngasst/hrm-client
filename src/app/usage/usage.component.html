How to Use This Tool

  1. Choose your environment the the menu on the top-right.

  2. Enter your search criteria, abiding by the following rules:

    • You may enter as many letters as you with the "Search By Table Name" input box

    • You may not use any spaces in the "Search By Table Name" input box. Please use underscores (_) as those are used in the HRM table names

    • You may use any case you with. lower_case, UPPER_CASE, mixed_CASE.

    • The above rules also apply for the "Search By Field Name" input box

    • Selecting either sorting order (alphabetic or highest row count firs) will NOT impact your results

    • Please be aware that unless you specifically request it, the checkbox to show empty tables is ticked off by default

  3. Press the "FILTER" button (or press th enter key while one of the two input boxes is highlight) and have a little patience while we fetch your results. :)

  4. If you entered something in the "Search By Table Name" input box, your search term will be highlighted in green.

  5. If you entered something in the "Search By Field Name" input box, your search term will be highlighted in blue

  6. If you wish to see sample entries for a selected table, follow these stemps.

    • Click on the table's name

    • A modal window will open. It will contain the first ten rows of that table.

    • Please give the modal window a moment to refresh. Even if it contains data, they might not belong to your table.

    • Don't worry, we're talking a second or two, here. Not the fifteen you're used to with HRM.

    • To close the modal window, simply click anywhere outside of it.

  7. Keep calm and love HRM
